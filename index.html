Project

My undergraduate project is "Design and fabrication of the model of smart wheelchair."

The common problem that has to be faced by paralyzed people is dependence of their mobility. They need someone’s help to do their daily activity. The main focus of this project is to provide these disabled persons by an automatic device with higher safety and at optimum cost. This smart wheelchair will operate based on the head movement or hand gesture or joystick movement of the operator. Using head movement or hand gesture or joystick movement the wheelchair will get continuous directions and speed command to steer this wheelchair. The development of smart wheelchair according to the design has been achieved using accelerometer sensor joystick module and microcontroller. It is user friendly and reduces the dependence of mobility of paralyzed people.

  • Introduction

    In developing countries disabilities is 10% (in percentage) of the total population. Considering the global statistics, the average number of peoples with disabilities would be at least about 15 million in Bangladesh. A smart wheelchair can be a solution for these physically handicapped people which will help them to reduce the limitation of their mobility. A smart wheelchair also concerns about the safety issue of the user. A smart wheelchair enables the operator to operate the wheelchair through head tilt, hand gesture or joystick. It can measure the blood pressure and send information about the emergency time.

  • Scope

    Physically Challenged patients can use this smart wheelchair.
    This smart wheelchair is multifunctional that’s why it may use widely in hospital.
    This smart wheelchair suits best at old rehabilitation center.

  • Advantages

    This smart wheelchair can be used by disabled persons as they want to use.
    People who are still able to moving their arm, they can use hand gesture or joystick.
    People who are too weak to move their arm, they can operate this wheelchair by tilting their head.
    It can avoid obstacles, that’s why it is more secured.
    It can measure blood pressure and alert at emergency time.

  • Disadvantage

    It can only be used in indoor only.
    Higher weight cannot be supported.
    It is not suitable for roads with higher slope.
    Low battery backup.

  • Future Enhancement

    • Solar panel can be the alternate power source.
    • Artificial intelligence (AI) technology can be implemented in this sector.
    • Using GPS navigation actual position of this device can be tracked.
    • Mind control operating wheelchair can be developed

  • Conclusion

    The primary concern of this project is to do something which will be helpful for mankind. Also to learn about robotic technology was another concern. Most of the modern technology uses accelerometer, ultrasonic sensor, gyroscope which are included in this project. So there is something to learn new from this project. Different technologies are used to make wheelchair more usable and comfortable for paralyzed people. Their dependent mobility will be reduced. An accelerometer is used to reduce physical hardship and this wheelchair can be operated using only hand gesture or head motion.
